You can’t go wrong with a hearty bowl of oatmeal especially during winter days , oatmeal will warm you and keep you full until lunch time and they are very easy to make , you don’t have to be good at cooking , they are very simple and you can choose your favourite kind of topping ,




How to make oatmeal in the microwave



1) Combine one cup of water or milk and 1/2 cup of oats and a pinch of a salt in a bowl , and microwave it on high for 1 minute , it depends on how you like your oatmeal to be like , you can microwave it for 2 to 3 minutes , but I personally like my oatmeal to be alittle raw and not over cooked

2) your oatmeal bowl will be ready after putting all your favourite topping such as fruits , nuts , peanut butter which I usually consider as an important ingredient in my oatmeal bowl For the oatmeal bowls in the photos , I used : Berries , peanut butter m sliced banana , pumpkin seeds , and I unusually use many kind of seeds so it can make my oatmeal bowl full of nutritions and healthy ingredients.
